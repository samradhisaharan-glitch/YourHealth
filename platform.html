Modules

Everything the platform contains.

The homepage keeps a short platform section; this page explains each module in more detail.

AI

AI symptom intake

Language-aware symptom capture, known-problem path, and structured care journey routing.

RA

Risk assessment

Priority tags, severity detection and red-flag review before booking.

DP

Department matching

Recommended department before doctor results, reducing patient confusion.

BK

Booking and payments

Real doctor availability, consultation fees, appointment slots and secure payment flow.

VC

Consultation

Video and chat consultation after booking, with patient context carried forward.

RX

Prescription and medicine

Doctor-generated prescriptions connected to pharmacy and ordering workflows.

IN

Insurance support

ABHA, PM-JAY, LIC health policy, coverage comparison and claim assistance.

HR

Digital records

Consultations, prescriptions, lab reports and claim documents in one patient timeline.

HM

Health monitoring

Follow-up reminders, vitals tracking and recovery tasks after consultation.
