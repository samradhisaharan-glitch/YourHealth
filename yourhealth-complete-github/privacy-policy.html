Privacy Policy

YourHealth India privacy policy.

This page explains how YourHealth India may collect, use, store and protect information for the website, app demo, doctor network, insurance support, digital records and contact forms.

Last updated: June 8, 2026. This policy is a product privacy draft for YourHealth India and should be reviewed by a qualified legal advisor before public launch or app store submission.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information provided by patients, doctors, partners or website visitors, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number and contact details.
  • Account signup information and authentication provider details.
  • Symptoms, health concerns, appointment details and consultation context.
  • Doctor profile details, specialisation, availability, consultation fee and payout details.
  • Insurance policy information, claim documents, cost estimation inputs and related support requests.
  • Digital records such as prescriptions, doctor-uploaded lab reports, consultation notes and monitoring reminders.

2. How We Use Information

We may use information to operate and improve YourHealth India, including to provide AI symptom intake, risk assessment, department recommendation, doctor booking, consultations, prescriptions, medicine ordering, insurance support, digital health records, monitoring and partner communication.

3. Health and Medical Data

Health-related data is sensitive. We aim to use it only for care coordination, patient records, insurance support, clinical workflows, monitoring and service improvement. YourHealth India is not a replacement for emergency medical care or direct medical advice from a qualified doctor.

4. Sharing Information

We may share relevant information only with parties needed to deliver the service, such as doctors, authorised clinical staff, hospitals, pharmacies, insurance partners, technology providers and support teams. We do not intend to sell personal health information.

5. Contact Form and Email

When you submit the contact form, your name, contact details, interest area and message may be sent to partneratyourhealthindia@gmail.com for follow-up.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information. No internet-based service can be guaranteed fully secure, so users should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive details through public forms.

7. Data Retention

We may retain information for as long as needed to provide services, maintain health records, comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, support insurance workflows and improve the platform.

8. User Choices

Users may request access, correction or deletion of their information by contacting YourHealth India. Some records may need to be retained where required for medical, legal, insurance or operational reasons.

9. Third-Party Services

The website or app may link to third-party services such as Gmail, Yahoo, LinkedIn, insurance partners, hospitals, pharmacies, hosting services and form submission providers. Their privacy policies apply separately.
