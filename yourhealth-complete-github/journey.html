The exact patient path inside YourHealth.

Every step moves the patient forward, while keeping the doctor, insurance and digital record workflows connected.

01

AI Symptom Checker

The patient describes symptoms or chooses a known problem. The app captures language, concern and urgency context.

Start

Risk Assessment Engine

The system checks severity, red flags and time sensitivity before moving the patient forward.

Safety
03

Department Recommendation

The app recommends General Physician, Cardiology, Dermatology, Psychiatry, Orthopaedics or another department.

Match
04

Appointment Booking

Only doctors who sign up and go live appear in the booking list. Patients choose slot, fee and mode.

Real doctors
05

Doctor Consultation

The patient connects with the doctor through video or chat, with consultation context already available.

Care

The doctor generates a digital prescription after consultation, with medicine, dosage and advice.

Rx

Medicine Ordering

The prescription can move into pharmacy fulfilment and medicine ordering workflows.

Pharmacy
08

Insurance Support

ABHA, PM-JAY, LIC health policy, cashless claims, reimbursement and cost estimation support the patient.

Coverage

Digital Health Records

Prescriptions, reports, consultation notes, claims and documents are saved in one patient timeline.

Records
10

Health Monitoring

Follow-up reminders, vitals and recovery tasks continue after the consultation and treatment.

Patients do not need to know the healthcare system. They start with symptoms and the app guides them step by step.
